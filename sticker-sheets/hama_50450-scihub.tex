%\input{filename} imports the commands from filename.tex into the target file; it's equivalent to typing all the commands from filename.tex right into the current file where the \input line is.

%\include{filename} essentially does a \clearpage before and after \input{filename}, together with some magic to switch to another .aux file.  \include{filename} gets you the speed bonus, but it also can't be nested, can't appear in the preamble, and forces page breaks around the included text.
